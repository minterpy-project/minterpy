chebyshev#

Implementations of the transformation classes from ChebyshevPolynomial (polynomials in the Chebyshev basis of the first kind) to:

  • LagrangePolynomial (polynomials in the Lagrange basis)

  • NewtonPolynomial (polynomials in the Newton basis)

  • CanonicalPolynomial (polynomials in the canonical basis)
